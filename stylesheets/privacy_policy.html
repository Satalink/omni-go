Privacy Policy for Omni-Go Extension App

Effective Date: August 12, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines the data practices for the Omni-Go Extension App ("Omni-Go"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains that Omni-Go does not collect, use, or store any personal data.

Data Collection

Omni-Go does not collect any personal or non-personal data from its users. The app operates locally on your device, and no information is gathered, processed, or transmitted to any servers or third parties.

Data Usage

Since Omni-Go does not collect any data, there is no usage of user data. The app functions solely to provide its features without accessing or utilizing any personal information.

Third-Party Services

Omni-Go does not integrate with or share data with any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks. No data is shared because no data is collected.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app’s functionality or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted at https://satalink.net/privacy-policy, and the effective date will be revised accordingly. Users are encouraged to review this policy periodically.
